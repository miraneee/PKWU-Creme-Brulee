Classic Crème Brûlée

Master the art of this elegant French dessert

Prep: 20 min
Cook: 45 min
Serves: 6
Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

For the Custard:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt

For the Caramelized Top:

  • 6 tbsp granulated sugar

Instructions

1

Prepare the Oven

Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C). Place 6 ramekins in a large baking dish or roasting pan.

2

Heat the Cream

In a medium saucepan, heat the heavy cream over medium heat until it just begins to simmer. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.

3

Mix the Custard Base

In a large bowl, whisk together egg yolks, 1/3 cup sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until well combined and slightly pale.

4

Temper the Eggs

Slowly pour the warm cream into the egg mixture, whisking constantly to prevent the eggs from scrambling. Strain the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve.

5

Fill the Ramekins

Divide the custard mixture evenly among the ramekins. Pour hot water into the baking dish until it reaches halfway up the sides of the ramekins.

6

Bake the Custards

Carefully transfer to the oven and bake for 40-45 minutes, until the centers are just set but still slightly jiggly.

7

Cool and Chill

Remove from water bath and let cool to room temperature. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.

8

Caramelize the Sugar

Just before serving, sprinkle 1 tablespoon of sugar evenly over each custard. Use a kitchen torch to caramelize the sugar until golden brown and crispy.

Pro Tips

Temperature Control

Use a kitchen thermometer to ensure the cream doesn't exceed 180°F when heating to prevent curdling.

Smooth Texture

Always strain the custard mixture to remove any lumps and ensure a silky smooth texture.

Perfect Caramelization

Move the torch in circular motions and keep it moving to avoid burning the sugar in one spot.

Timing is Key

Caramelize the sugar just before serving to maintain the crispy texture contrast.

Flavor Variations

Vanilla Bean

Replace vanilla extract with 1 vanilla bean pod, scraped, for intense vanilla flavor and beautiful specks.

Chocolate

Add 2 oz melted dark chocolate to the warm cream for a rich chocolate crème brûlée.

Lavender

Infuse the cream with 1 tsp dried culinary lavender for a floral, sophisticated twist.

Coffee

Add 2 tbsp instant espresso powder to the cream for a coffee lover's delight.
